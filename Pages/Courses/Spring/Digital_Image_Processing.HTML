Digital Image Processing

Course Contents:

Intorduction to DIP (Image sources, acquisition and representation, Key stages in DIP), Human vision, Image interpolation (pixel replication, nearest neighbor interpolation, comparison of interpolations), Steganography Neighborhood (4,8 and diagonal) & connectivity (4 , 8 & m connectivity) Paths and regions, CC labeling, Image enhancement, Contrast stretching, histogram equalization, Spatial filtering, Spatial differentiation, Laplacian filter and enhancement, Usage of gradient for image enhancement (Sobel & Prewitt operator), Morphological Image processing, set theory, hit and fit of structuring element, Erosion and dilation, opening and closing, Image Segmentation, Detection of Discontinuities, Point Detection, Line Detection, Edge Detection, Edge Linking and Boundary Detection, Local Processing, Thresholding, Basic Global Thresholding, Basic Adaptive Thresholding, Local Thresholding, Region Growing, Region Splitting and Merging
